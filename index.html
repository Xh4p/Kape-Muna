🌿 Our Story

Born on the Island

Nestled on the serene shores of Talim Island, Kape Muna started as a dream — a cozy corner where the community could gather, sip handcrafted frappes, and feel the warm breeze of island life.

Every drink is crafted with love using premium ingredients that bring out rich, layered flavors of café culture mixed with island-inspired creativity.
